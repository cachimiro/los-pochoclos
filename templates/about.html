Los Pocholos, located at 7 minute drive from Mingeuo, near Palomino. 2 hrs by bus from Santa Marta or 1.5 hrs from Riohacha. The finca is a comfortable house with large bedrooms set in a garden; both fan and air conditioned options are available,. The ocean (a kilometer of totally unspoilt Caribbean beach fringed with palm trees) is 100 metres away, a place where you can sit back and enjoy roaring waves at night, the river running on one side of the finca with fisherman passing twice a day thet deliver local fresh fish. There is a cook who serves delicious and inexpensive fresh meals, including fresh fish, plaintains, and fruit juices from the garden.
